Privacy Policy

  • I do not want your data, and I do the absolute minimum with it that I have to, to be able to provide the features I do want.
  • I do not collect any personally identifiable data, beyond normal website traffic logging. If you arrived here via a link which contains data, I will be able to see that and where you came from, but that's all.
  • I use cookies to make some features of the site "sticky" so you don't have to reset them every time you visit. These cookies contain no information beyond the ID and the setting, and I do not share them with anyone.
  • Your data is stored locally, in your browser's data store, except for Google Drive™ data (see below). It is not transmitted to the server, and I will not be able to see it.
  • Google Drive activity happens entirely between your browser and Google servers. I never see any of that traffic, nor do I transmit any of that information to my server.
  • I do my best never to access any data on your Google Drive not created by this application. You can verify this by enabling your browser's debug console and following the traffic.
  • If you're really interested in what this does, read the source. It's on GitHub. The relevant files you want to look at are:
    • Drive.js
    • _cloud.js
