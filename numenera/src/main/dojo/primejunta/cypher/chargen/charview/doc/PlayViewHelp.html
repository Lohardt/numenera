Play View

This is a simple tool for displaying character data and tracking changes to pools while you play.

Background and notes

You can make changes to your background and notes. Changes will get written back to the character sheet, and they will get saved when you switch between characters.

In-play adjustments

Use the and buttons to adjust your stats. They will be disabled if you hit zero or your character's maximum.

XP: XP will be written back to your character sheet when you close the Play View. If the control is grayed out, you haven't finalized your character yet with the Advance button. Go back to the main view and do that, then try again.

Effort: Set your level of effort from the Effort control at top. Then click next to the stat for which you want to use it. The effort will be deducted from the stat, with Edge taken into account.

Damage track: Moves up and down if any of your pools are at zero.

Recovery: The recovery button is enabled (and turns blue) if your stats fall below their maximums. It cycles through A, 10 m, 1 h, and 10 h depending on your next available recovery roll. The points will be distributed between your pools in an attempt to keep them relatively even. If you don't like this, adjust by hand with the and buttons but don't cheat or your GM will get mad.

Note: The utility does not account for the Special Healing needed by a hero who Fuses Flesh and Steel.

Abilities

Your abilities are displayed in the ability pane.

Inventory

Your inventory is displayed in the inventory pane.

Party

Pick your party members from the Party pane. Switch between them with the buttons in the heading. Changes made in Play View will be saved when you switch.
